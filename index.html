About Me

I started my journey by making terracotta jewelry using a special kind of mud called Pokhran mitti from Rajasthan. I joined the NCUI incubation center and eventually became the treasurer of a small Self-Help Group, where my work received more support and recognition. Along with my team, I create beautiful, handmade jewelry that celebrates the richness of Indian culture.
